How it works

Three steps, zero trusted third parties.

1

You submit a claim

Free-form text or a URL + question about it. Your wallet signs a single transaction; nothing is held off-chain.

2

Validators fact-check independently

Each validator runs a fresh LLM completion with the same prompt. They cannot see each other's output. They often use different underlying models (Gemini, GPT, Kimi…).

3

On-chain consensus

GenLayer's prompt_comparative equivalence principle requires all validators to agree on the VERDICT. If one disagrees, the tx reverts.
