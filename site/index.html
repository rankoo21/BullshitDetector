How it works

1

You submit a claim

Free-form text or a URL. Your wallet signs a transaction; nothing is held off-chain.

2

Five validators fact-check independently

Each validator runs a fresh LLM completion with the same prompt. They cannot see one another's output.

3

On-chain consensus

GenLayer's prompt_comparative equivalence principle requires all validators to agree on VERDICT. Confidence may vary by ≤25 points.
